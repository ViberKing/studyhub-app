Academic integrity agreement

Please read carefully before using the Research Assistant.

Permitted uses

  • ✓ Organising your reading materials
  • ✓ Understanding complex texts
  • ✓ Creating study materials from sources you've read
  • ✓ Finding relevant sections to read in detail
  • ✓ Literature review preparation

Prohibited uses

  • ✗ Using summaries instead of reading the actual sources
  • ✗ Copying summaries directly into essays
  • ✗ Paraphrasing AI summaries as your own writing
  • ✗ Submitting work without reading the originals
  • ✗ Failing to cite sources properly

Declaration

I understand St Andrews' academic integrity policies.
I will use summaries only to guide my reading.
All submitted work will be my own original writing.
I will properly cite all sources in my essays.
I will read the actual sources, not rely on summaries alone.

St Andrews policy: Academic misconduct includes plagiarism, contract cheating, and submitting work that is not your own. Consequences range from grade penalties to expulsion. If in doubt, contact your module coordinator or Student Learning Development.
